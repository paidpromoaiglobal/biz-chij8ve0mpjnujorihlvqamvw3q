Welcome to Sharmi Parlour & Makeover Artist, nestled in the heart of Nehru Nagar, Velachery, Chennai. We are a trusted beauty destination known for our warm hospitality, skilled hands, and a genuine passion for making every client look and feel their absolute best. From everyday grooming to special occasion makeovers, our experienced team — led by the beloved Kalai Akka — is dedicated to delivering results that exceed your expectations.

Whether you're stepping in for a relaxing head massage, a rejuvenating facial, precise eyebrow shaping, or a full bridal makeover, Sharmi Parlour offers personalized care with a gentle touch.
